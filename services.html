Our Services

Medico-Legal Advice

We can provide medico-legal reports for clinical negligence, personal injury
and criminal cases within 4-6 weeks of receiving the document bundle.
Our areas of expertise include:

  • Breast Cancer
  • Lung Cancer
  • Urological cancers including Prostate cancer
  • Colorectal Cancer
  • Gynaecological cancers
  • Hematological cancers
  • Upper GI cancers, including gastric and oesophageal

Pagination Services

At your request, our Oncology Nurse Specialist can:

  • Re-organise medical records and produce an index;
  • Paginate medical records; and
  • Produce chronologies, summarising key events.

Medical Literature Services

At your request, our oncology librarian can:

  • Search for published literature on any cardiovascular topic;
  • Obtain the published literature;
  • Review the the published literature;
  • Produce a report on the published literature; and
  • Provide the published literature with a full list of references.

Medical Illustration Services

At your request, our medical artists can produce:

  • Diagrams to illustrate cardiovascular pathologies or treatments;
  • Search libraries for diagrams, figures or photographs that illustrate
    cardiovascular disease, injuries or treatments;
  • Take medical photographs for use in medico-legal reports.

Medical Statistics Services

At your request, our medical statistician can produce:

  • Actuarial calculations, with respect to life expectancy,
    for use in medico-legal reports.
